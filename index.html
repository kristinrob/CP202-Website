Harry James Potter

Harry is known as The Boy Who Lived, he is the only person to survive what's known as the Killing Curse, which gave him his signature lighening bolt scar. He is a memeber of the Gryffindor house and his patronus, or spell to ward off Dementors, which are the guardians of prison Azkaban, is a stag. The core of his wand is a pheonix feather. He used to live with his Muggle, meaning person who is a non-wizard) Aunt and Uncle Dursley and his cousin Dudley before he discovered he was a wizard when he turned eleven. He is best friends with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.

Ron Weasley

Ron Weasley is a wizard and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He is a pure-blood wizard, meaning both of his parents were Wizards. He was sorted into Gryffindor house when he was first admitted to the school like his friends Harry, Hermione and Neville Longbottom. His patronus is a Jack Russell Terrier and his wand core is a unicorn tail hair. He lives with his mother and father as well as his many siblings, including twins Fred and George and sister Ginny. He is best friends with Harry Potter and Hermione Granger.

Hermionie Granger

Hermione is a wizard and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry! She is known for her smarts and enuthuiasm to learn which profesors love but many other students do not. She is a muggle-born wizard, meaning both of her parents are not wizards, which she is regliarly bullied for from other Hogwarts students. She was sorted into Gryffindor house when she was first admitted to the school. Her patronus is an otter and her wand core is a dragon heartstring. She is best friends with Harry Potter and Ron Weasley!
