Harry Potter


Harry is known as The Boy Who Lived, as he is the only person to survive what's known as the Killing Curse, which gave him his signature lighening bolt scar. He is a member of the Gryffindor house and his patronus, or spell to ward off Dementors, which are the guardians of the prison Azkaban, is a stag. The core of his wand is a pheonix feather. He used to live with his Muggle, meaning person who is a non-wizard, Aunt and Uncle Dursley and his cousin Dudley before he discovered he was a wizard when he turned eleven. He is best friends with fellow wizards Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.

Ron Weasley


Ron Weasley is a wizard and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He is a pure-blood wizard, meaning both of his parents were Wizards. He was sorted into Gryffindor house when he was first admitted to the school like his friends Harry, Hermione and Neville Longbottom. His patronus is a Jack Russell Terrier and his wand core is a unicorn tail hair. He lives with his mother and father as well as his many siblings, including twins Fred and George and sister Ginny. He is best friends with Harry Potter and Hermione Granger.

Hermione Granger


is a wizard and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry! She is known for her smarts and enuthuiasm to learn which profesors love but many other students do not. She is a muggle-born wizard, meaning both of her parents are not wizards, which she is regliarly bullied for from other Hogwarts students. She was sorted into Gryffindor house when she was first admitted to the school. Her patronus is an otter and her wand core is a dragon heartstring. She is best friends with Harry Potter and Ron Weasley!

Albus Dumbledore


is the Headmaster of the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He was considered to be one of the most powerful wizards of his time. Dumbledore watched over Harry Potter after Harry's parents died, helping him to become a great wizard and preparing him for his future battle against Lord Voldemort. Dumbledore's patronus is a pheonix and his wand core is a Thestral tail hair core. He belongs to the Gryffindor house.

Severus Snape


Snape is a professor at the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. When Harry first entered the school, Professor Snape was the potions professor and the head of the Slytherin House. Later, Professor Snape was the professor for defense against the dark arts class. Snape is especially tough on Harry when he first enters Hogwarts. Snape's patronus is a doe and he belongs to the Slytherin house.

Lord Voldemort


Also known as The Dark Lord and "he who shall not be named", is considered to be one the of the most powerful and dangerous dark wizards of all time. Voldemort is the leader of the Death Eaters and caused the First Wizarding War. He killed Harry Potter's parents, James and Lily, before trying to kill Harry. He lost the majority of his power when The Killing Spell, meant to kill Harry backfired and weakened Voldemort and cause him to flee. He was part of the Slytherin house when he was a student at Hogwarts school where he was known as Tom Marvolo Riddle.

Who is J.K. Rowling?

Rowling wrote the first Harry Potter novel in various cafes throughout Endinburgh. The first Harry Potter novel was submitted to thirteen publishing houses, all of which rejected the manuscript except one. The decision to publish Rowling's book owes much to the eight-year-old daughter of the publisher's chairman, who was given the first chapter to review by her father and immediately demanded the next.

The Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

The Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is located in Scotland, United Kingdom. The school has multiple charms and spells on and around it that make it impossible for a Muggle to locate it. The castle's setting is described as having extensive grounds with sloping lawns, flowerbeds and vegetable patches, a loch called The Black Lake, a large dense forest called the Forbidden Forest, several greenhouses and other outbuildings, and a full-size Quidditch pitch. Quiddich is a famous wizarding sport played on broomsticks. It is the most popular and well-known game among wizards and is Howgarts' most honourable sport. There is also an owlery, which houses all the owls owned by the school and those owned by students. Some rooms in the school tend to "move around", and so do the stairs in the grand staircase. The main way to get to Hogwarts from the Muggle world is the Hogwarts Express that students take at the start of each school year. Students board the train from the hidden Platform 9 3/4 at King's Cross station in London.

The Houses of Hogwarts

ogwarts students are sorted into 1 of 4 houses based on their personalities, courage, bravery and more. What house a student is apart of represents who they are and what they value.
